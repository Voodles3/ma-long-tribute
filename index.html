Ma Long

The greatest table tennis player of all time

Ma Long is a Chinese professional table tennis player, six-time Olympic champion, and three-time World Champion. He is widely regarded as the best to ever play the sport, having held the world #1 ranking for a total of 64 months (and 34 consecutively), the most by any man in the history of table tennis. He is also the first and only male player to achieve a career Double Grand Slam.

On December 31st, 2024, at the age of 36, Ma Long withdrew from ITTF world rankings, seeming to indicate his semi-retirement from professional competition. He has had a profound impact on the sport of table tennis, and his legacy will be remembered for generations to come.
